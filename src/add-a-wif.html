The Rules

Wif explanations must be understandable by an intelligent person with little-to-no knowledge of technology. Imagine you're writing for an average Sociology professor.
  • Reader must not need to google anything to understand your explanation.
  • Explanation should convey a high-level understanding of the problem the technology solves.
  • Explanation must present at least 1 real-world example of when the technology would be useful.
  • Explanation must provide link(s) to further reading that are NOT linking to technical documentation.


After you submit, I'll do my best to review your submission within a few days.
