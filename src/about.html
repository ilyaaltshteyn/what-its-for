About

This is a place to find no-background-knowledge-required explanations of code libraries and software.

Why? Because every time I dive into a new area, I re-experience the pain of having to learn the lingo before being able to understand what the simplest things are.

When you google "YAML", the most prominent result comes from Wikipedia. It explains that "YAML is a human-readable data-serialization language." Those intimidating, obscure words "data-serialization language" kept me from looking any further for 5 years. When I finally gathered the courage to dive into the explanation, I found out that YAML is just a standardized format for storing data, similar to JSON.

Simple explanations of most technical concepts are possible. I hope that WIF can give them a home.
